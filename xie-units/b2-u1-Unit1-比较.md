## Unit1 比较

## 比较变化

为了表示性质、程度等差异，形容词在字形上所做的变化，称为比较。比较分为原级、比较级、最高级。

(1)规则变化

<table><tr><td>级\变化</td><td>原级</td><td>比较级</td><td>最高级</td></tr><tr><td>1.原级+er, est</td><td>short</td><td>shorter</td><td>shortest</td></tr><tr><td>2.原级字尾有e+r,st</td><td>nice</td><td>nicer</td><td>nicest</td></tr><tr><td>3.原级为短母音+子音→重复字尾+er, est</td><td>hot</td><td>hotter</td><td>hottest</td></tr><tr><td>4.原级字尾为子音+y→去y+ier, iest</td><td>easy</td><td>easier</td><td>easiest</td></tr><tr><td>5.两音节以上的形容词→more, most+原级</td><td>beautiful</td><td>morebeautiful</td><td>mostbeautiful</td></tr></table>


(2) 不规则变化


<table><tr><td>原级</td><td>比较级</td><td>最高级</td></tr><tr><td>bad(坏的) ill(生病)</td><td>worse</td><td>worst</td></tr><tr><td>good(好的) well(健康)</td><td>better</td><td>best</td></tr><tr><td>many(指可数的) much(指不可数的)</td><td>more</td><td>most</td></tr><tr><td>little(少的)</td><td>less</td><td>least</td></tr><tr><td>far(远的)</td><td>farther further</td><td>farthest(表距离) furthest(表程度,数量,距离)</td></tr><tr><td>late(晚的;迟的)</td><td>later latter</td><td>latest(表时间) last(表顺序)</td></tr><tr><td>old(老的;旧的)</td><td>older elder</td><td>oldest(表年纪;新旧) eldest(表长幼)</td></tr></table>


(例)1.He was happy in his later life.


(他晚年很快乐。)

2. As for French and German, the latter is more difficult for me than the former. 

(就法文和德文而言，我觉得后者比前者难。)

<a id="b2-u1-形容词的比较级"></a>

## 形容词的比较级

(1) 比较级...than...

(例) 1. Your article is longer than mine.

(你的文章比我长。)

2. Mary is more beautiful than she/her. (口语)

(玛丽比她漂亮。)

(2) the+比较级...of the two...

(例)1. Kevin is the older of the two boys.

(Kevin是两位男孩中年纪较大的。)

2. Jack is the more active of the twins. 

(Jack 是这对双胞胎中较活跃的。)

(3)修饰比较级

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l}\mathrm{much,a lot,far + 比较级}\rightarrow \dots \text{得多了}\\ \mathrm{even + 比较级}\rightarrow \dots \text{更加}\\ \mathrm{a little + 比较}\rightarrow \dots \text{一点} \end{array} \right.$ 

(例)1.He is much busier than I.

(他比我忙碌得多了。)

2. This is a little cheaper than that. 

(这个比那个便宜一点。)

3. The price of the blue pants is far higher than that of the green pants. 

(蓝裤子的价格比绿裤子的价格高得多。)

4. The weather in Kaohsiung is far hotter than that in Taipei. 

=The weather is far hotter in Kaohsiung than in Taipei. 

(高雄的天气比台北的天气更加炎热。)

(注意)比较时，若主题相同或语意上清楚明白时，than以下部分可省略。

(例)They live a more pleasant life than (they did) before.

(他们现在比以前过著更愉快的生活。)

(4)比较级...to...

junior(年幼的)、senior(年长的)、major(大的)、minor(小的)等形容词，在表示比较的对象时，不用than而用to。

(例)She is two years older than $\left\{\begin{array}{l}I.\\me.\end{array}\right.$ 

=She is senior to me by two years. 

(她比我大2岁。)

(5)比较级的惯用表现

a. 比较级 and 比较级 越来越...

(例)The story became more and more interesting.

(这故事变得越来越有趣。)

b. the 比较，the比较级 越...越...

例)1.The more, the better.(越多越好。)

2. The more we get, the happier we'll be. 

(我们得到越多就越快乐。)

c. $\left\{\begin{array}{l} more than 超过 \\ less than 少于 \end{array}\right.$ 

(例)The man is $\left\{\begin{array}{c} more than \\ over \end{array}\right\}$ eighty years old.

(这个人超过80岁。)

<a id="b2-u1-形容词的最高级"></a>

## 形容词的最高级

(1)~the 最高级+名词+ $\left\{\begin{array}{l}\text { 地点 } \\ \text { of the three... } \\ \text { of all }\end{array}\right.$ 

(例)1.Helen is the best student of all.

(海伦是所有学生中最好的。)

2.Mt.Everest is the highest mountain in the world. 

(圣母峰是世界上最高的山。)

(2)原级、比较级、最高级互换

(例)1.Taipei is the biggest city in Taiwan.

=Taipei is bigger than any other city in Taiwan. 

=Taipei is bigger than all the other cities in Taiwan. 

=No other city in Taiwan is bigger than Taipei. 

=No other cities in Taiwan are as big as Taipei. 

(台北是台湾最大的城市。)

(注意)加other(其他的)，其功用为避免和本身做比较。

2. { New York is bigger than any other city in America.
(纽约比美国任何其他城市大。)
New York is bigger than any city in Taiwan.
(纽约比台湾任何城市大。)

(3)as 原级 as → 像...一样

(例)1.This jacket is as expensive as this sweater.

(这件夹克和这件毛衣一样贵。)

2. Your hair is as long as mine.. 

(你的头发和我的一样长。)

(注意)否定句时，也可用not $\left\{\begin{array}{l} so \\ as \end{array}\right\} \sim as...$ 

(例)This question is not $\left\{\begin{array}{l} as \\ so \end{array}\right\}$ difficult as it seems.

(这问题并不像表面上的那么困难。)

## 副词的比较级、最高级

## 比较变化

a规则变化→和形容词一样，由字形的变化而来。

<table><tr><td></td><td>原级</td><td>比较级</td><td>最高级</td></tr><tr><td>副词字尾无ly→加er,est</td><td>fast</td><td>faster</td><td>fastest</td></tr><tr><td>副词字尾有ly→more,most+副词</td><td>quickly</td><td>more quickly</td><td>most quickly</td></tr></table>

<table><tr><td>原级</td><td>比较级</td><td>最高级</td></tr><tr><td>badly(坏)ill(不好)</td><td>worse</td><td>worst</td></tr><tr><td>well(好)</td><td>better</td><td>best</td></tr><tr><td>much(多)</td><td>more</td><td>most</td></tr><tr><td>little(少)</td><td>less</td><td>least</td></tr><tr><td rowspan="2">far(远)</td><td>farther</td><td>farthest(表距离)</td></tr><tr><td>further</td><td>furthest(表程度)</td></tr></table>

◎副词的比较级+than

(例)1.He can sing better than Lisa.

(他可以唱的比丽莎好。)

2.I study harder than my friends. 

(我比我的朋友们更努力。)

◎the 副词的最高级+ $\left\{\begin{array}{l} 地点 \\ of the three \end{array}\right\}\rightarrow$ 副词的最高级，the可以省略。

(例)1.My father gets up (the)earliest of us all.

(我父亲是我们之中最早起的。)

2. Cathy dances (the) most beautifully. 

(凯西是跳舞跳得最美的。)

(注意)形容词和副词的不同

例) $\left\{\begin{aligned}&Tom is the fastest boy of all.\rightarrow 形容词的最高级，the不可省略。\\&(汤姆是所有男孩中最快的。)\end{aligned}\right.$ $Tom runs (the)fastest of all.\rightarrow 副词的最高级，the可以省略。$ $(汤姆是所有男孩中跑得最快的。)$ 

## ◎疑问词+比较

（例）1.Which fruit do you like better, apples or oranges?

(你比较喜欢哪一种水果，苹果或是柳橙？)

2. Which do you like (the) best, apples, oranges or peaches? 

(苹果、柳橙和桃子，你最喜欢哪一种？)

(注意)两者之间用比较级；三者(或以上)用最高级。
