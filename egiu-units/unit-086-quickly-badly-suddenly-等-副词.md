# Unit 86 quickly/badly/suddenly 等 (副词)

## good（形容词）→ well（副词）

□ Your English is very good.

□ You speak English very well. (而不用 very good)

□ It was a good game.

但 well 也可作形容词（表示“身体健康的”）：

□ 'How are you?' 'I'm very well, thank you. And you?'
