# Unit 24 规则动词与不规则动词

## 规则动词与不规则动词

## 规则动词

规则动词的过去式与过去分词以 -ed 结尾：

clean → cleaned live → lived paint → painted study → studied

过去式（参见 Unit 11）：

□ I cleaned my room yesterday.

□ Charlie studied engineering at university.

## 过去分词：

现在完成时为 have/has + 过去分词（参见 Units 15-18）:

□ I have cleaned my room.

□ Tina has lived in London for ten years.

被动语态为 be (is/are/were/has been 等) + 过去分词 (参见 Units 21-22):

☐ These rooms are cleaned every day.

□ My car has been repaired.

## 不规则动词

不规则动词的过去式与过去分词不是以 -ed 结尾：

<table><tr><td>make</td><td>break</td><td>cut</td></tr><tr><td>made</td><td>broke</td><td>cut</td></tr><tr><td>made</td><td>broken</td><td>cut</td></tr></table>

有些动词的过去式与过去分词相同，例如：

<table><tr><td>make</td><td>find</td><td>buy</td><td>cut</td></tr><tr><td>made</td><td>found</td><td>bought</td><td>cut</td></tr></table>

□ I made a cake yesterday. (过去式)

☐ I have made some coffee. (过去分词：现在完成时)

□ Butter is made from milk. (过去分词：一般现在时的被动语态)

有些动词的过去式与过去分词不同，例如：

<table><tr><td>break</td><td>know</td><td>begin</td><td>go</td></tr><tr><td>broke</td><td>knew</td><td>began</td><td>went</td></tr><tr><td>broken</td><td>known</td><td>begun</td><td>gone</td></tr></table>

□ Somebody broke this window last night. (过去式)

□ Somebody has broken this window. (过去分词：现在完成时)

☐ This window was broken last night. (过去分词：一般过去时的被动语态)
