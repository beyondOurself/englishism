## ago

ago 表示 “之前，以前”：

□ Susan started her new job three weeks ago. (= three weeks before now)

□ 'When did Tom go out?' 'Ten minutes ago.' (= ten minutes before now)

□ I had dinner an hour ago.

□ Life was very different a hundred years ago.

ago 与动词的过去式 (started/did/had/was 等) 连用。

请比较 ago 与 for:

□ When did Jane arrive in Ireland?
She arrived in Ireland three days ago.

□ How long has she been in Ireland? She has been in Ireland for three days.

## I did（一般过去时）比较

与动作完成的时间 (yesterday / last week 等) 连用时，用一般过去时 (arrived/saw/was 等):

一般过去时 + 动作完成的时间

过去 现在

不要将现在完成时 (have arrived / have done / have been 等) 与动作完成的时间连用:

□ I saw Paula yesterday. (而不用 I have seen)

□ Where were you on Sunday afternoon? (而不用 Where have you been)

□ We didn't have a holiday last year. (而不用 We haven't had)

□ 'What did you do last night?' 'I stayed at home.'

□ William Shakespeare lived from 1564 to 1616. He was a writer. He wrote many plays and poems.

可用一般现在时询问 When ...? 或 What time ...? 类型的问题：

□ When did you buy your computer? (而不用 When have you bought)

□ What time did Andy go out? (而不用 What time has Andy gone out?)
