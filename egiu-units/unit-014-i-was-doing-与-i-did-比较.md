## I was doing（过去进行时）与 I did（一般过去时）比较

Jack was reading a book.

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/ab6a0be1d838c98a.jpg)


The phone rang.


![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/67e3af9f3ec77e37.jpg)


He stopped reading.


![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/5e7e93ec41a24432.jpg)


He answered the phone.


What happened? The phone rang. (一般过去时)

What was Jack doing when the phone rang? He was reading a book. (过去进行时)

What did he do when the phone rang?
He stopped reading and answered the phone. } (一般过去时)

☐ A: What did you do yesterday morning?
B: We played tennis (from 10 to 11.30).

## 过去进行时

10 o'clock

☐ A: What were you doing at 10.30?
B: We were playing tennis.
