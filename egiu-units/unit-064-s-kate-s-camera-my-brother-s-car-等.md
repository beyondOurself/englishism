# Unit 64 -'s (Kate's camera / my brother's car 等)

## -'s (Kate's camera / my brother's car 等)

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/dc906ce19633df36.jpg)


Kate's camera (her camera)


![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/d846ad4c0ea13b5c.jpg)


my brother's car (his car)


![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/861ac1844dfdf946.jpg)


the manager's office (his 或 her office)


-’s 通常用于人：
□ I stayed at my sister's house. (而不用 the house of my sister)
□ Have you met Mr Black's wife? (而不用 the wife of Mr Black)
□ Are you going to James's party?
□ Paul is a man's name. Paula is a woman's name.

-’s 后不一定带有名词：
□ Sophie's hair is longer than Kate's. (= Kate's hair)
□ ‘Whose umbrella is this?’ ‘It's my mother's.’ (= my mother's umbrella)
□ ‘Where were you last night?’ ‘I was at Paul's.’ (= Paul's house)
