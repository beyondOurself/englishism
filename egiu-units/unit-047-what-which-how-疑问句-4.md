# Unit 47 What ...? Which ...? How ...? (疑问句 4)

## Unit 47

## What ...? (疑问句 4)

## Which ... ? How ... ?

what + 名词 (What colour ... ? / What kind ... ? 等)

□ What colour is your car?

□ What colour are your eyes?

□ What size is this shirt?

□ What make is your TV set?

□ What time is it?

□ What day is it today?

□ What kind of job do you want? (或 What type of job ... ? / What sort of job ... ?)

what 后可不带名词：

□ What's your favourite colour?

□ What do you want to do tonight?

## which + 名词（物或人）:

□ Which train did you catch – the 9.50 or the 10.30?

□ Which doctor did you see – Doctor Ellis, Doctor Gray or Doctor Hill?

which 后不带名词时指代物，不指代人：

□ Which is bigger – Canada or Australia?

who 指代人，后面不带名词：

□ Who is taller – Joe or Gary? (而不用 Which is taller?)

## what 与 which 比较

which 表示可能的选择较少（或许有 2 个、3 个或 4 个选择）：

□ We can go this way or that way.

Which way shall we go?

☐ There are four umbrellas here.
Which is yours?

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/e469dd8a1fea8f8d.jpg)


what 表示的范围则更广：

WHICH?

□ What's the capital of Argentina? (在阿根廷的所有城市中)

□ What sort of music do you like? (在所有种类的音乐中)

请比较：

□ What colour are his eyes? (而不用 Which colour?)

Which colour do you prefer, pink or yellow?

□ What is the longest river in the world?

Which is the longest river – the Mississippi, the Amazon or the Nile?

## How ...?

□ 'How was the party last night?' 'It was great.'

□ 'How do you usually go to work?' 'By bus.'

可以用 how + 形容词 / 副词 (how tall / how old / how often 等):
