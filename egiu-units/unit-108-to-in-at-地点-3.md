# Unit 108 to in at (地点 3)

## arrive 与 get 比较

arrive in 后接国家或城镇 (arrive in Italy / arrive in Paris 等):

□ They arrived in England last week. (而不用 arrived to England)

arrive at 后接其他地点 (arrive at the station / arrive at work 等):

□ What time did you arrive at the hotel? (而不用 arrive to the hotel)

get to 后接地点：

□ What time did you get to the hotel?

□ What time did you get to Paris?

get home / arrive home (不带介词):

□ I was tired when I got home. 或 I was tired when I arrived home.

## next to / beside / between / in front of / behind

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/2a3daa9424c4dc3a.jpg)


A is next to B. 或 A is beside B.
B is between A and C.
D is in front of B.
E is behind B.

也可以说：
A is on the left.
C is on the right.
B is in the middle (of the group).
