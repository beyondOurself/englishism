## friend's 与 friends' 比较：

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/00dc6f5c77aeebf0.jpg)


my friend's house 一个朋友的房子 (= his house or her house)

's 用于 friend/student/mother 等（单数）之后：

my mother's car (母亲一个人)
my father's car (父亲一个人)

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/cc27522b22c0faa9.jpg)


my friends' house 两个或多个朋友的房子 (= their house)


'用于 friends/students/parents 等（复数）
之后：
my parents' car（父母亲两个人）


of ... 用于物体、地点等：

- Look at the roof of that building. (而不用 that building's roof)
- We didn't see the beginning of the film. (而不用 the film's beginning)
- What's the name of this village?
- Do you know the cause of the problem?
- You can sit in the back of the car.
- Madrid is the capital of Spain.

a ... 表示 “一个（事物或人）”：
□ Rachel works in a bank. (而不用 in bank)
□ Can I ask a question? (而不用 ask question)
□ I haven't got a computer.
□ There's a woman at the bus stop.

元音 a/e/i/o/u 前用 an (而不用 a):
□ Do you want an apple or a banana?
□ I'm going to buy a hat and an umbrella.
□ There was an interesting programme on TV last night.

要说 an hour（因为 h 不发音）
a university（因为 university 的读音以辅音开头）
a European country（因为 European 的读音以辅音开头）

another (= an + other) 是一个词：
□ Can I have another cup of coffee?

介绍某事物或某人时，要用 a/an ...:
□ The sun is a star.
□ Football is a game.
□ Dallas is a city in Texas.
□ A mouse is an animal. It's a small animal.
□ Joe is a very nice person.

a/an ... 可修饰职业等：
□ A: What's your job?
B: I'm a dentist. (而不用 I'm dentist)
□ 'What does Mark do?' 'He's an engineer.'
□ Would you like to be a teacher?
□ Beethoven was a composer.
□ Picasso was a famous painter.
□ Are you a student?

## 名词的复数形式通常以 -s 结尾：

单数（一个） → 复数（两个或多个）

a flower → some flowers

a train → two trains

one week → a few weeks

a nice place → some nice places

this student → these students

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/f6575c7f6fa1b745.jpg)

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/9e3ad409b686dcbe.jpg)

## 拼写（参见附录5）：

-s / -sh / -ch / -x → -es bus → buses dish → dishes

church → churches

box → boxes

另 potato → potatoes

tomato → tomatoes

-y → -ies baby → babies

dictionary → dictionaries

party → parties

但 -ay / -ey / -oy → -ys day → days

-f / -fe → -ves

shelf → shelves

knife → knives

boy → boys

wife → wives
