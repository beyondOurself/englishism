# Unit 60 my/his/their 等

## my/his/their 等

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/0099eff9fbf6cbd3.jpg)


my umbrella

our umbrella

your umbrella

his umbrella

her umbrella

their umbrella

I → my

I like my house.

we → our

We like our house.

you → your

You like your house.

he → his

He likes his house.

she → her

She likes her house.

they → their

They like their house.

it → its

Oxford (= It) is famous for its university.

## its 与 it's 比较

Oxford is famous for its university.

it's (= it is) I like Oxford. It's a nice place. (= It is a nice place.)
