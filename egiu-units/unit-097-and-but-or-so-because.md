# Unit 97 and but or so because

and but or so because

这些连词用来连接两个句子，从而将两个短句并为一个长句：

A句

The car stopped.

The driver got out.

B句

The car stopped and the driver got out.

## and/but/or

<table><tr><td>A句</td><td>B句</td></tr><tr><td>We stayed at home</td><td>and (we)★ watched television.</td></tr><tr><td>My sister is married</td><td>and (she)★ lives in London.</td></tr><tr><td>He doesn't like her,</td><td>and she doesn't like him.</td></tr><tr><td>I bought a newspaper,</td><td>but I didn't read it.</td></tr><tr><td>It's a nice house,</td><td>but it hasn't got a garden.</td></tr><tr><td>Do you want to go out,</td><td>or are you too tired?</td></tr></table>

★ 不必重复 we 与 she

当多个短句并列时，用逗号(,)连接前几项，并用 and 连接最后两项：

□ I got home, had something to eat, sat down in an armchair and fell asleep.

□ Karen is at work, Sue has gone shopping and Chris is playing football.

so（表结果）

<table><tr><td>A句</td><td>B句</td></tr><tr><td>It was very hot,so</td><td>I opened the window.</td></tr><tr><td>Joe does a lot of sport,so</td><td>he's very fit.</td></tr><tr><td>They don't like travelling,so</td><td>they haven't been to many places.</td></tr></table>

## because (表原因)

<table><tr><td colspan="2">A句</td><td>B句</td></tr><tr><td>I opened the window</td><td>because</td><td>it was very hot.</td></tr><tr><td>Joe can't come to the party</td><td>because</td><td>he's going away.</td></tr><tr><td>Lisa is hungry</td><td>because</td><td>she didn't have breakfast.</td></tr></table>

## because 也常用于句首：

□ Because it was very hot, I opened the window.

下列例句中有不止一个连词：

☐ It was late and I was tired, so I went to bed.

□ I always enjoy visiting London, but I wouldn't like to live there because it's too big.
