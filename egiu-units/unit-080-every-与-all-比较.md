# Unit 80 every 与 all 比较

## every

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/962d6b4d331421da.jpg)


Every house in the street is the same.

every house in the street = all the houses in the street

## every 之后跟单数名词 (every house / every country 等):

□ Sarah has been to every country in Europe.

□ Every summer we have a holiday by the sea.

□ She looks different every time I see her.

every ... 之后的谓语动词用单数形式:

□ Every house in the street is the same. (而不用 are the same)

□ Every country has a national flag. (而不用 have)

## 请比较 every 与 all:

□ Every student in the class passed the exam.

□ Every country has a national flag.

□ All the students in the class passed the exam.

□ All countries have a national flag.

## every day 与 all day 比较

every day 表示 “每天”

how often?

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/c7bc867736fac0dc.jpg)


all day 表示 “一整天”

EVERY DAY

☐ It rained every day last week.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/18d7a359110999a4.jpg)


□ Bill watches TV for about two hours every evening. (= on all evenings)

□ It rained all day yesterday.

也可用 every morning/night/summer 等。

☐ On Monday, I watched TV all evening.
(= the complete evening)

也可用 all morning/night/summer 等。

## everybody (或 everyone) / everything / everywhere

everybody 或 everyone

(人物)

□ Everybody (或 Everyone) needs friends.

everything

(= all people need friends)

(事物)

□ Have you got everything you need?

everywhere

(= all the things you need)

(地点)

□ I lost my watch. I've looked everywhere for it.

(= I've looked in all places)

everybody/everyone/everything 后用动词单数形式：

□ Everybody has problems. (而不用 Everybody have)
