# Unit 101 a person who ... (定语从句 1)

## a person who ... (定语从句 1)

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/7bbc5fcd3d0c40c7.jpg)


![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/20839ffd0453ed3e.jpg)


I met a woman. She can speak six languages.
—— 两句

she → who

I met a woman who can speak six languages.

Jack was wearing a hat. It was too big for him.
—— 两句 ———

it → that 或 which

Jack was wearing a hat that was too big for him.
或

Jack was wearing a hat which was too big for him.

who 用于指人（而非物）:

## that 用于指物或指人均可：

<table><tr><td>An aeroplane is a machineEmma lives in a houseThe people</td><td>that flies.that is 400 years old.that work in the office</td><td>are very friendly.</td></tr></table>

that 可用于指人，但 who 更常见些。

which 用于指物（而非人）:

<table><tr><td>An aeroplane is a machineEmma lives in a house</td><td>which flies. (而不用 a machine who ...)which is 400 years old.</td></tr></table>

which 不用于指人:
□ Do you remember the woman who was playing the piano at the party?
(而不用 the woman which ...)

## 101.2 将括号中的两个句子并为一句。

1 (A man phoned. He didn't give his name.) The man who phoned didn't give his name.

2 (A woman opened the door. She was wearing a yellow dress.) The woman

3 (Some students took the exam. Most of them passed.) Most of the students

4 (A policeman stopped our car. He wasn't very friendly.) The
