## quickly 与 suddenly 为副词。

形容词 + -ly → 副词：

形容词
副词

quick quickly bad

badly

sudden

suddenly

拼写（参见附录5）：

easy → easily

careful

carefully

heavy → heavilyheavy
heavily 等

## 副词用来讲述某事如何发生或某人如何做某事：

□ The train stopped suddenly.

□ I opened the door slowly.

□ Please listen carefully.

□ I understand you perfectly.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/55a1a258b91d86cd.jpg)

□ I felt nervous.

□ Listen carefully! (而不用 listen careful)

□ Our team played badly. (而不用 played bad)

## C

## hard fast late early

这些单词既是形容词又是副词：

□ Sue's job is very hard.

□ Sue works very hard. (而不用 hardly)

□ Ben is a fast runner.

□ The bus was late/early.

□ Ben can run fast.

□ I went to bed late/early.
