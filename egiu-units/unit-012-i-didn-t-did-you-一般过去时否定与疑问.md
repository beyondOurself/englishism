# Unit 12 I didn't ... Did you ...? (一般过去时否定与疑问)

## I didn't ... Did you ... ?

## (一般过去时的否定式与疑问式)

## A 一般过去时的否定式与疑问式中用 did:

## 动词原形 肯定式

<table><tr><td>playstartwatchhaveseedoogo</td><td>Iweyoutheyhesheit</td><td>playedstartedwatchedhadsawsdidwent</td><td>Iweyoutheyhesheit</td><td>did not(didn’t)</td><td>playstartwatchhaveseedogo</td><td>did</td><td>Iweyoutheyhesheit</td><td>play?start?watch?have?see?do?go?</td></tr></table>

## do/does (现在) → did (过去):

□ I don't watch television very often.

I didn't watch television yesterday.

□ Does she often go away?

Did she go away last week?

## 要用 did/didn't + 动词原形 (watch/play/go 等):

<table><tr><td>I watchedthey wenthe hadyou did</td><td>但用 I didn't watchdid they go?he didn't havedid you do?</td><td>(而不用 I didn't watched)(而不用 did they went?)</td></tr></table>

□ I played tennis yesterday, but I didn't win.

□ 'Did you do the shopping?' 'No, I didn't have time.'

□ We went to the cinema, but we didn't enjoy the film.

请看一般过去时疑问式的词序：

did + 主语 + 动词原形

## 简略答语

<table><tr><td>Yes,</td><td>I/we/you/they he/she/it</td><td>did.</td><td>No,</td><td>I/we/you/they he/she/it</td><td>didn't.</td></tr></table>

□ 'Did you see Joe yesterday?' 'No, I didn't.'

□ 'Did it rain on Sunday?' 'Yes, it did.'

□ 'Did Helen come to the party?' 'No, she didn't.'

□ 'Did your parents have a good holiday?' 'Yes, they did.'
