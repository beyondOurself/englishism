# Unit 33 I have to ...

## must 与 have to 比较

must 或 have to 可用来表示认为有必要做某事或提出个人看法：

□ It's a fantastic film. You must see it. 或 You have to see it.

不是表达个人看法时用 have to (而不用 must)。请比较：

□ Jane won't be at work this afternoon. She has to go to the doctor.

(不是表达个人看法，而是陈述事实)

□ Jane isn't well. She doesn't want to go to the doctor, but I told her she must go. (是表达个人看法)
