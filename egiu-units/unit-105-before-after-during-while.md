## during, while 与 for 比较

during 之后接名词 (during the film), while 之后接句子 (while I'm reading):

□ We didn't speak during the meal.

或 We didn't speak while we were eating. (而不用 during we were eating)

for (而不用 during) 之后接某一段时间 (three days / two hours / a year 等):

□ We played tennis for two hours. (而不用 during two hours)

□ I lived in London for a year. (而不用 during a year)

before/after 之后可接动词 -ing 形式 (before going / after eating 等):

☐ I always have breakfast before going to work. (= before I go to work)

□ After doing the shopping, they went home. (= after they did)

注意要说 before going (而不用 before to go), after doing (而不用 after to do) 等:

Before eating the apple, I washed it carefully. (而不用 before to eat)

☐ I started work after reading the newspaper. (而不用 after to read)
