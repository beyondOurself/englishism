5 Our house is number 45 – the number is ____ the door.

6 'Is the post office near here?' 'Yes, turn left ____ the traffic lights.'

7 It's difficult to park ____ the centre of town. It's better to take the bus.

8 My sister lives .... Brussels.

9 There's a small park .... the top of the hill.

10 I think I heard the doorbell. There's somebody .... the door.
