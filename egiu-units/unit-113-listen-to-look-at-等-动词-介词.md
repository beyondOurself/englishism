## C

## depend

可以说 depend on ...:

□ A: Do you like eating in restaurants?

B: Sometimes. It depends on the restaurant. (而不用 it depends of)

it depends what/where/how（等）之后用不用 on 均可：

□ A: Do you want to come out with us?

B: It depends where you're going. 或 It depends on where you're going.
