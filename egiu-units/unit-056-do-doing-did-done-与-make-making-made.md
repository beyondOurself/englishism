# Unit 56 do/doing/did/done 与 make/making/made

## get in/out/on/off

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/1963e404039014ac.jpg)


get in (a car)


get out (of a car)


get off


(a bus / a train / a plane)


- Kate got in the car and drove away. (也可说: Kate got into the car and ...)
- A car stopped and a man got out. (也可说: A man got out of the car.)
- We got on the bus outside the hotel and got off in Church Street.
