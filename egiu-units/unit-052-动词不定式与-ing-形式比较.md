# Unit 52 动词不定式与 -ing 形式比较

# 动词不定式（I want to do）与 -ing 形式（I enjoy doing）比较

## 动词 + 不定式 (I want to do)

want plan

decide try

hope expect

need promise

+ to ... (to do / to work / to be 等)

□ What do you want to do this evening?

□ It's not very late. We don't need to go home yet.

□ Tina has decided to sell her car.

☐ You forgot to switch off the light when you went out.

□ My brother is learning to drive.

□ I tried to read my book, but I was too tired.

## B 动词 + -ing 形式 (I enjoy doing)

suggest

+ -ing (doing / working / being 等)

□ I enjoy dancing. (而不用 enjoy to dance)

□ I don't mind getting up early.

□ Has it stopped raining?

□ Sonia suggested going to the cinema.

I enjoy dancing.

## 动词 + -ing 形式或不定式

continue

+ -ing (doing 等) 或 to ... (to do 等)

□ Do you like getting up early? 或 Do you like to get up early?

□ I prefer travelling by car. 或 I prefer to travel by car.

□ Anna loves dancing. 或 Anna loves to dance.

□ I hate being late. 或 I hate to be late.

□ It started raining. 或 It started to rain.

## D would like to ... 等

+ to ... (to do / to work / to be 等)

□ Julia would like to meet you.

□ I'd love to go to Australia. (I'd = I would)

□ 'Would you like to sit down?' 'No, I'd prefer to stand, thank you.'

□ I like this city very much. I wouldn't like to move.

□ I'd hate to lose my address book.
