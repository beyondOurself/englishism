# Unit 27 will/shall (1)

## shall

可以用 I shall (= I will) 与 we shall (= we will):
□ I shall be late tomorrow. 或 I will (I'll) be late tomorrow.
□ I think we shall win. 或 I think we will (we'll) win.

在构成将来时的时候，不可将 shall 与 you/they/he/she/it 等非第一人称连用：
□ Tom will be late. (而不用 Tom shall be)

## 27.5 从画线部分中选出正确的表达方式。(参见 Unit 25。)

1 We'll go / We're going to the theatre tonight. We've got tickets. (We're going is right)
2 'What will you do / are you doing tomorrow evening?' 'Nothing. I'm free.'
3 They'll go / They're going away tomorrow morning. Their train is at 8.40.
4 I'm sure your aunt will lend / is lending us some money. She's very rich.
5 'Why are you putting on your coat?' 'I'll go / I'm going out.'
6 Do you think Claire will phone / is phoning us tonight?
7 Steve can't meet us on Saturday. He'll work / He's working.
8 Will you / Shall you be at home tomorrow evening?
9 A: What are your plans for the weekend?
B: Some friends will come / are coming to stay with us.

I'll (I will) ... 可表示提议或决定做某事：
□ 'My bag is very heavy.' 'I'll carry it for you.'
□ 'I'll phone you tomorrow, OK?' 'OK, bye.'

I think I'll ... / I don't think I'll ... 可表示决定做某事：
□ I'm tired. I think I'll go to bed early tonight.
□ It's a nice day. I think I'll sit outside.
□ It's raining. I don't think I'll go out.

有些句子中不用一般现在时 (I go / I phone 等)，例如：
□ I'll phone you tomorrow, OK? (而不用 I phone you)
□ I think I'll go to bed early. (而不用 I go to bed)

I'll ... 不用来表示事先决定好的事情 (参见 Units 25-26):
□ I'm working tomorrow. (而不用 I'll work)
□ There's a good film on TV tonight. I'm going to watch it. (而不用 I'll watch)
□ What are you doing at the weekend? (而不用 What will you do)
