## make 与 let 的用法

在 make 和 let 后面，不可使用 to:

□ He's very funny. He makes me laugh. (而不用 makes me to laugh)

□ At school our teacher made us work very hard.

□ Sue let me use her computer because mine wasn't working. (而不用 let me to use)

当想要某人与你一起做某事时，可以用 Let's ... (= Let us):

□ Come on! Let's dance.

□ 'Do you want to go out tonight?' 'No, I'm tired. Let's stay at home.'
