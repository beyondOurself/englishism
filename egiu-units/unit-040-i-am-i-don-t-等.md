# Unit 40 I am, I don't 等

## did 表示一般过去时（参见 Unit 12）：

□ A: Did you and Chris enjoy the film?

B: I did, but Chris didn't. (= I enjoyed it, but Chris didn't enjoy it)

□ 'I had a good time.' 'I did too.' (= I enjoyed it too)

□ 'Did it rain yesterday?' 'No, it didn't.'

40.1 完成下列句子，要求每次只用一个动词（is/have/can 等）。
1 Kate wasn't hungry, but we ____ . 4 I haven't seen the film, but Tom .....
2 I'm not married, but my brother .... . 5 Karen won't be here, but Chris .....
3 Bill can't help you, but I .... . 6 You weren't late, but I .....
40.2 用动词的否定式（isn't/haven't/can't 等）完成下列句子。
1 My sister can play the piano, but I ____ . 4 Mark has been to China, but I .....
2 Sam is working today, but I .... . 5 I'm ready to go, but Tom .....
3 I was working, but my friends .... . 6 I've got a key, but Sally .....
40.3 用 do/does/did 或 don't/doesn't/didn't 完成下列句子。
1 I don't like hot weather, but Sue ____ .
2 Sue likes hot weather, but I ____ .
3 My mother wears glasses, but my father .....
4 You don't know Paul very well, but I .....
5 I didn't enjoy the party, but my friends .....
6 I don't watch TV much, but Peter .....
7 Kate lives in London, but her parents .....
8 You had breakfast this morning, but I .....
40.4 仿照示例，根据你自己与别人的情况完成句子。
1 I didn't ____ go out last night, but my friends did.
2 I like ____, but .....
3 I don't ____, but .....
4 I'm .....
5 I haven't .....
40.5 用动词的肯定式或否定式填空。
1 'Are you tired?' 'I ____ earlier, but I'm not now.'
2 Steve is happy today, but he ____ yesterday.
3 The post office isn't open yet, but the shops .....
4 I haven't got a telescope, but I know somebody who .....
5 I would like to help you, but I'm afraid I .....
6 I don't usually go to work by car, but I ____ yesterday.
7 A: Have you ever been to the United States?
B: No, but Sandra ____ . She went there on holiday last year.
8 'Do you and Chris watch TV a lot?' 'I ____ , but Chris doesn't.'
9 I've been invited to Sam's wedding, but Kate .....
10 'Do you think Sarah will pass her driving test?' 'Yes, I'm sure she ____.'
11 'Are you going out tonight?' 'I ____ . I don't know for sure.'
40.6 根据你自己的情况，用 Yes, I have. / No, I'm not. 等回答下列问题。
1 Are you American? No, I'm not.
2 Have you got a car?
3 Do you feel OK?
4 Is it snowing?
5 Are you hungry?
6 Do you like classical music?
7 Will you be in Paris tomorrow?
8 Have you ever broken your arm?
9 Did you buy anything yesterday?
10 Were you asleep at 3 a.m.?
