# Unit 93 He speaks English very well. (词序 1)

## He speaks English very well. (词序 1)

谓语动词 + 宾语

动词 (reads) 与宾语 (a newspaper) 常连用。
通常说：

□ Sue reads a newspaper every day.
(而不用 Sue reads every day a newspaper)

## 地点 + 时间

地点状语 (where?) 常置于时间状语 (when?) 之前。通常说：

□ We went to a party last night. (而不用 We went last night to a party)

## A 下列词语 (always/never 等) 常与动词连用，位于句子中间：

<table><tr><td>always</td><td>often</td><td>ever</td><td>rarely</td><td>also</td><td>already</td><td>all</td></tr><tr><td>usually</td><td>sometimes</td><td>never</td><td>seldom</td><td>just</td><td>still</td><td>both</td></tr></table>

□ My brother never speaks to me.

□ She's always late.

□ Do you often go to restaurants?

□ I sometimes eat too much. (或 Sometimes I eat too much.)

□ 'Don't forget to phone Laura.' 'I've already phoned her.'

□ I've got three sisters. They're all married.

## always/never 等用于动词之前：

动词

<table><tr><td>always often never等</td><td>go play have等</td></tr></table>

□ I always drink coffee in the morning.

(而不用 I drink always coffee)

□ Helen often goes to London.
(而不用 Helen goes often)

□ You sometimes look unhappy.

□ They usually have dinner at 7 o'clock.

□ We rarely (或 seldom) watch television.

☐ Richard is a good footballer. He also plays tennis and volleyball.
(而不用 He plays also tennis)

□ I've got three sisters. They all live in London.

## 但 always/never 等用于 am/is/are/was/were 之后：

□ I am always tired. (而不用 I always am tired)

always □ They are never at home during the day.

often □ It is usually very cold here in winter.

never □ When I was a child, I was often late for school.

□ 'Where's Laura?' 'She's still in bed.'

□ I've got two brothers. They're both doctors.
