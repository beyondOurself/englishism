# Unit 30 can 与 could 比较

<table><tr><td>I/we/you/they he/she/it</td><td>can can't (cannot)</td><td>do play see come 等</td><td>can</td><td>I/we/you/they he/she/it</td><td>do? play? see? come? 等</td></tr></table>

I can (do something) 表示 “知道如何（做某事）或有可能（做某事）”：

□ I can play the piano. My brother can play the piano too.

□ Sarah can speak Italian, but she can't speak Spanish.

□ 'Can you swim?' 'Yes, but I'm not a very good swimmer.'

□ 'Can you change twenty pounds?' 'I'm sorry, I can't.'

□ I'm having a party next week, but Paul and Rachel can't come.

表示过去 (yesterday / last week 等) 时，用 could/couldn't:

□ When I was young, I could run very fast.

Before Maria came to Britain, she couldn't understand much English. Now she can understand everything.

□ I was tired last night, but I couldn't sleep.

□ I had a party last week, but Paul and Rachel couldn't come.

Can you ... ? Could you ... ? Can I ... ? Could I ... ?

Can you ...? 或 Could you ...? 用于请求对方做某事：

□ Can you open the door, please? 或 Could you open the door, please?

□ Can you wait a moment, please? 或 Could you wait ... ?

Can I have ...? 或 Could I have ...? 用于向对方要求某物：

☐ (in a shop) Can I have these postcards, please? 或 Could I have ...?

Can I ... ? 或 Could I ... ? 用于向对方征询意见：

□ Tom, can I borrow your umbrella? 或 Tom, could I borrow your umbrella?

□ (on the phone) Hello, can I speak to Gary, please? 或 ... could I speak ...?

must + 动词原形 (must do / must work 等):

<table><tr><td>I/we/you/they he/she/it</td><td>must</td><td>dogoseeat等</td></tr></table>

I must (do something) 表示 “我有必要（做某事）”：

□ I'm very hungry. I must eat something.

□ It's a fantastic film. You must see it.

☐ The windows are very dirty. We must clean them.

表示过去 (yesterday / last week 等) 时，用 had to ... (而不用 must):

□ I was very hungry. I had to eat something. (而不用 I must eat)

□ We had to walk home last night. There were no buses. (而不用 We must walk)

## mustn't (= must not)

I mustn't (do something) 表示 “我一定不要，千万不要（做某事）”：

□ I must hurry. I mustn't be late.

□ I mustn't forget to phone Jane.

(= I must remember to phone her)

□ Be happy! You mustn't be sad. (= don't be sad)

□ You mustn't touch the pictures. (= don't touch the pictures)

You mustn't touch the pictures.
