# Unit 3 I am doing (现在进行时)

## I am doing（现在进行时）

I'm eating.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/9ed42341dd2f8b9e.jpg)


She's eating.
She isn't reading.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/52e3447ed6f067ec.jpg)


It's raining.
The sun isn't shining.

We're running.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/3022cdf5438332ad.jpg)


They're running.
They aren't walking.


现在进行时的形式为：

am/is/are + doing/eating/running/writing 等。

<table><tr><td>I</td><td>am (not)</td><td rowspan="3">-ing</td></tr><tr><td>he she it</td><td>is (not)</td></tr><tr><td>we you they</td><td>are (not)</td></tr></table>

□ I'm working. I'm not watching TV.

□ Maria is reading a newspaper.

□ She isn't eating. (或 She's not eating.)

□ The phone is ringing.

□ We're having dinner.

□ You're not listening to me. (或 You aren't listening ...)

☐ The children are doing their homework.

am/is/are + -ing 表示 “现在正在发生某事”：

I'm working

she's wearing a hat

they're playing football

I'm not watching television

过去

现在

将来

□ Please be quiet. I'm working. (= I'm working now)

□ Look, there's Sarah. She's wearing a brown coat. (= she is wearing it now)

□ The weather is nice. It's not raining.

□ 'Where are the children?' 'They're playing in the park.'

□ (on the phone) We're having dinner now. Can I phone you later?

□ You can turn off the television. I'm not watching it.

拼写（参见附录5）：

come → coming

write → writing

run → running

dance → dancing

sit → sitting

swim → swimming

lie → lying

am/is/are → Unit 1 are you doing? (疑问式) → Unit 4 I am doing 与 I do 比较 → Unit 8 What are you doing tomorrow? → Unit 25
