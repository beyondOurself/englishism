I → my → mine

we → our → ours

you → your → yours

he → his → his

she → her → hers

they → their → theirs

It's my money.

It's mine.

It's our money. It's ours.

It's his money. It's his.

It's her money. It's hers.

It's their money. It's theirs.

## my/your 等 + 名词 (my hands / your book 等):

□ My hands are cold.

□ Is this your book?

□ Helen gave me her umbrella.

□ It's their problem, not our problem.

## mine/yours 等后面不跟名词：

□ Is this book mine or yours? (= my book or your book)

□ I didn't have an umbrella, so Helen gave me hers. (= her umbrella)

□ It's their problem, not ours. (= not our problem)

☐ We went in our car, and they went in theirs. (= their car)

## his 后带不带名词均可：

□ 'Is this his camera or hers?' 'It's his.'

## a friend of mine / a friend of his / some friends of yours 等

□ I went out to meet a friend of mine. (而不用 a friend of me)

□ Tom was in the restaurant with a friend of his. (而不用 a friend of him)

□ Are those people friends of yours? (而不用 friends of you)

## Whose ... ?

□ Whose book is this? (= Is it your book? his book? my book? 等)

## whose 之后带不带名词均可：

□ Whose money is this?

Whose is this?

□ Whose shoes are these?

Whose are these?

It's mine.

They're John's.

61.1 用 mine/yours 等完成句子。
1 It's your money. It's yours....5 It's their house. It's .....
2 It's my bag. It's ....6 They're your books. They're .....
3 It's our car. It's ....7 They're my glasses. They're .....
4 They're her shoes. They're ....8 It's his coat. It's ....

1 It's their/theirs problem, not our/ours. (their and ours are right)
2 This is a nice camera. Is it your/yours?
3 That's not my/mine umbrella. My/Mine is black.
4 Whose books are these? Your/Yours or my/mine?
5 Catherine is going out with her/hers friends this evening.
6 My/Mine room is bigger than her/hers.
7 They've got two children, but I don't know their/theirs names.
8 Can we use your washing machine? Our/Ours isn't working.
