# the people we met the hotel you stayed at (定语从句 2)

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/70053b012644963e.jpg)


![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/a3c34ddfdc4af740.jpg)


The man is carrying a bag.
It's very heavy. } 两句

The bag (that) he is carrying is very heavy.
一句

Kate won some money.
What is she going to do with it?} 两句

What is Kate going to do with the money (that) she won? 一句

可以说：

☐ The bag that he is carrying ... 或 The bag he is carrying ... (用不用 that 均可)

□ ... the money that Kate won? 或 ... the money Kate won?

指代宾语时，that/who/which 可省略：

<table><tr><td>主语</td><td>谓语动词</td><td>宾语</td><td></td></tr><tr><td>The man</td><td>was carrying</td><td>a bag</td><td>→ the bag (that) the man was carrying</td></tr><tr><td>Kate</td><td>won</td><td>some money</td><td>→ the money (that) Kate won</td></tr><tr><td>You</td><td>wanted</td><td>some books</td><td>→ the books (that) you wanted</td></tr><tr><td>We</td><td>met</td><td>some people</td><td>→ the people (who) we met</td></tr></table>

□ Did you find the books you wanted? (或 ... the books that you wanted?)

☐ The people we met were very friendly. (或 The people who we met ...)

□ Everything I said was true. (或 Everything that I said ...)

注意：

☐ The film we saw was very good. (而不用 The film we saw it was ...)

有时谓语动词之后跟介词 (to/in/at 等):

<table><tr><td>Eve is talking to a man.</td><td>→</td><td>Do you know the man Eve is talking to?</td></tr><tr><td>We stayed at a hotel.</td><td>→</td><td>The hotel we stayed at was near the station.</td></tr><tr><td>I told you about some books.</td><td>→</td><td>These are the books I told you about.</td></tr></table>

注意：

... the books I told you about. (而不用 the books I told you about them)

可以说 (a place) where ...:

☐ The hotel where we stayed was near the station. (= The hotel we stayed at ...)

指代主语时，who/that/which 不能省略（参见 Unit 101）：

□ I met a woman who can speak six languages. (who 为主语)

□ Jack was wearing a hat that was too big for him. (that 为主语)

## 通常说：

at the weekend

at night

□ Are you going away at the weekend?

at Christmas / at Easter

□ I can't sleep at night.

at the end of ...

□ Where will you be at Christmas? (或 on Christmas Day)

at the moment

□ I'm going on holiday at the end of October.

□ Are you busy at the moment?

## in the morning / in the afternoon / in the evening

□ I always feel good in the morning.

但用

□ Do you often go out in the evening?

## on Monday morning / on Tuesday afternoon / on Friday evening / on Saturday night 等：

□ I'm meeting Joanne on Monday morning.

□ Are you doing anything on Saturday night?

## at/on/in 不用于下列短语之前：

this ... (this morning / this week 等)

last ... (last August / last week 等)

next ... (next Monday / next week 等)

every ... (every day / every week 等)

□ Are you going out this evening?

□ We go on holiday every summer.
Last summer we went to Canada.

□ I'm leaving next Monday.
(而不用 on next Monday)
