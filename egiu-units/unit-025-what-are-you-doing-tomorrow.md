# Unit 25 What are you doing tomorrow?

## What are you doing tomorrow?

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/7d5753d512fc9efa.jpg)


They are playing tennis (now).

today is Sunday

I'm playing tennis tomorrow.

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/8315fddaf98fd92f.jpg)


He is playing tennis tomorrow.


am/is/are + -ing（现在进行时）可用来表示“现在正在发生某事”：

□ 'Where are Sue and Amanda?' 'They're playing tennis in the park.'

□ Please be quiet. I'm working.

am/is/are + -ing 也可用来表示将来 (tomorrow / next week 等):

□ Andrew is playing tennis tomorrow.

□ I'm not working next week.

I am doing something tomorrow 表示 “我已安排好明天要做某事 / 我计划明天做某事”

- Sophie is going to the dentist on Friday. (= she has an appointment with the dentist)
- We're having a party next weekend.
- Are you meeting your friends tonight?
- What are you doing tomorrow evening?
- I'm not going out tonight. I'm staying at home.

也可说 I'm going to do something (参见 Unit 26)。

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/53845679abecb364.jpg)


要注意，不用一般现在时 (I stay / do you go 等) 表示计划与安排：

□ I'm staying at home this evening. (而不用 I stay)

□ Are you going out tonight? (而不用 Do you go)

□ Lisa isn't coming to the party next week. (而不用 Lisa doesn't come)

但用一般现在时表示时间表、节目单、火车与公共汽车时刻表等：

□ The train arrives at 7.30.

□ What time does the film finish?

请比较：

现在进行时——通常指人

一般现在时——指时间表、节目单等

□ I'm going to a concert tomorrow.

□ What time are you leaving?

□ The concert starts at 7.30.

□ What time does your train leave?
