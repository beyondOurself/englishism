# Unit 5 I do/work/like 等 (一般现在时)

## I do/work/like 等（一般现在时）

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/2387db552bb6373f.jpg)


They're looking at their books. They read a lot.

![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/beyondOurself/englishism/main/assets/egiu/0dacb1c023bd0e4b.jpg)


He's eating an ice-cream. He likes ice-cream.

they read / he likes / I work 等为一般现在时：

<table><tr><td>I/we/you/they</td><td>read</td><td>like</td><td>work</td><td>live</td><td>watch</td><td>do</td><td>have</td></tr><tr><td>he/she/it</td><td>reads</td><td>likes</td><td>works</td><td>lives</td><td>watches</td><td>does</td><td>has</td></tr></table>

he works / she lives / it rains 等:
☐ I work in a shop. My brother works in a bank. (而不用 My brother work)
☐ Lucy lives in London. Her parents live in Scotland.
☐ It rains a lot in winter.
I have → he/she/it has:

I have → he/she/it has:
□ John has a shower every day.

拼写（参见附录5）：

-es 用于 -s / -sh / -ch 之后： pass → passes finish → finishes watch → watches
-y → -ies: study → studies try → tries
还有： do → does go → goes

一般现在时可用来表示一般性的事实、有时或经常发生的事情：
□ I like big cities.
□ Your English is good. You speak very well.
□ Tim works very hard. He starts at 7.30 and finishes at 8 o'clock in the evening.
□ The earth goes round the sun.
□ We do a lot of different things in our free time.
□ It costs a lot of money to build a hospital.

always/never/often/usually/sometimes 常与一般现在时连用：
□ Sue always gets to work early. (而不用 Sue gets always)
□ I never eat breakfast. (而不用 I eat never)
□ We often go away at weekends.
□ Mark usually plays football on Sundays.
□ I sometimes walk to work, but not very often.

I don't ...（否定式）→ Unit 6 Do you ...? （疑问式）→ Unit 7 I am doing 与 I do 比较 → Unit 8 always/usually/often 等（词序）→ Unit 94
