# put your shoes on

## (短语动词 2)

有时短语动词 (put on / take off 等) 带宾语。例如：

动词 宾语

put on your coat

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/3ca2c5644ccb93e1.jpg)


或 put your coat on动词 宾语
take off your shoes

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/afbedecaa0687be9.jpg)


可以说：take off your shoes或 take your shoes off

但 it/them（代词）作宾语时，总是用于 on/off 等之前：
put it on (而不用 put on it) take them off (而不用 take off them)

□ It was cold, so I put on my coat.
(或 I put my coat on)

□ Here's your coat. Put it on.

□ Your shoes are dirty. Take them off.

## 类似的还有：

turn on / turn off (灯、机器、龙头等):

□ It was dark, so I turned on the light.
(或 I turned the light on)

□ I don't want to watch this programme. You can turn it off.

也可用 switch on / switch off (灯、机器等):

TURN OFF

□ I switched on the light and switched off the television.

pick up / put down:

☐ Those are my keys on the floor. Can you pick them up for me?

□ I stopped reading and put my book down.
