## 简略答语

<table><tr><td>I/we/you/they do.</td></tr><tr><td>he/she/it does.</td></tr></table>

<table><tr><td rowspan="2">No,</td><td>I/we/you/they don't.</td></tr><tr><td>he/she/it doesn't.</td></tr></table>

□ 'Do you play tennis?' 'No, I don't.'

□ 'Do your parents speak English?' 'Yes, they do.'

□ 'Does Gary work hard?' 'Yes, he does.'

□ 'Does your sister live in London.' 'No, she doesn't.'
