# I don't ... (一般现在时的否定式)

## 一般现在时的否定式为 don't/doesn't + 动词：

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/3f3da5526a15b560.jpg)


She doesn't drink coffee.

![image](https://gitee.com/shencanlong/englishism/raw/main/assets/egiu/6936d0c9d36144bf.jpg)


He doesn't like his job.

□ I drink coffee, but I don't drink tea.

□ Sue drinks tea, but she doesn't drink coffee.

□ You don't work very hard.

□ We don't watch television very often.

□ The weather is usually nice. It doesn't rain very often.

□ Gary and Nicole don't know many people.

## 记住：

I/we/you/they don't ... □ I don't like football.
he/she/it doesn't ... □ He doesn't like football.

□ I don't like Fred and Fred doesn't like me. (而不用 Fred don't like)

□ My car doesn't use much petrol. (而不用 My car don't use)

□ Sometimes he is late, but it doesn't happen very often.

要用 don't/doesn't + 动词原形 (don't like / doesn't speak / doesn't do 等，而不用其他形式):

□ I don't like washing the car. I don't do it very often.

□ Sarah speaks Spanish, but she doesn't speak Italian. (而不用 doesn't speaks)

□ Bill doesn't do his job very well. (而不用 Bill doesn't his job)

□ Paula doesn't usually have breakfast. (而不用 doesn't ... has)
