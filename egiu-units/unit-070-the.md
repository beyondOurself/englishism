# Unit 70 the ...

## Unit 70

## the

当说话人和听话人明白所谈及的人或物时，要用 the:
□ What is the name of this street? (这条街只有一个名称)
□ Who is the best player in your team? (全队只有一个最佳球员)
□ Can you tell me the time, please? (= the time now)
□ My office is on the first floor. (= the first floor of the building)

注意不能省略 the:
□ Do you live near the city centre? (而不用 near city centre)
□ Excuse me, where is the nearest bank? (而不用 where is nearest ...)

## the same

□ We live in the same street. (而不用 in same street)
□ 'Are these two books different?' 'No, they're the same.' (而不用 they're same)

## 可以说：

the sun / the moon / the world / the sky / the sea / the country
□ The sky is blue and the sun is shining.
□ Do you live in a town or in the country?

the police / the fire brigade / the army (of a city, country 等)
□ My brother is a soldier. He's in the army.
□ What do you think of the police? Do they do a good job?

下列词语前不用 the:

television / TV

□ I watch TV a lot.

□ What's on television tonight?

但 Can you turn off the television? (= the TV set)

breakfast / lunch / dinner

□ What did you have for breakfast? (而不用 the breakfast)
□ Dinner is ready!

next / last + week/month/year/summer/Monday 等
□ I'm not working next week. (而不用 the next week)
□ Did you have a holiday last summer? (而不用 the last summer)
