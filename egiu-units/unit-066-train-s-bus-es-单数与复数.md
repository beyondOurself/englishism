# Unit 66 train(s) bus(es) (单数与复数)

## 有些单词的复数形式不是以 -s 结尾：

this man → these men

a woman → some women

one foot → two feet

that sheep → those sheep

a child → many children

a tooth → all my teeth

a fish → a lot of fish

a mouse → some mice

此外还有 a person → two people / some people / a lot of people 等：

□ She's a nice person.

但 □ They are nice people. (而不用 nice persons)

people 是复数 (= they), 因此用 people are / people have 等:

□ A lot of people speak English. (而不用 speaks)

□ I like the people here. They are very friendly.

police 是复数：

☐ The police want to talk to anybody who saw the accident. (而不用 The police wants)

by → Units 21, 63, 109 at/on → Units 103, 106–107 介词 + -ing → Unit 112
